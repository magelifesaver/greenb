== Description ==

Transform your inventory management with our Barcodes add-on. Easily create barcodes for products, orders, suppliers, and more. Customize barcodes and take advantage of Multi-Inventory support. Improve operations with barcode integration in list tables, purchase order emails, PDFs, and more. Enhance efficiency and accuracy in inventory tracking today.
